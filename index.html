Daily Wellness Tip

Take a deep breath and focus on the present moment.

Mindfulness & Relaxation Activities

  • 4-7-8 Breathing (inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8)
  • Box Breathing (inhale, hold, exhale, hold - each for 4 counts)
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing
  • Basic Yoga Poses (Downward Dog, Child's Pose)
  • Body Scan Meditation
  • Mindful Eating (focus on each bite and the flavors)
  • Walking in Nature (focus on the sights, sounds, and smells)
  • Outdoor Meditation (sit in a quiet spot outside)
  • Sun Salutation at Sunrise or Sunset
