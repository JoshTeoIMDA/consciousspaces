Mental Health Organizations in Singapore

Contact local mental health organizations for support and resources.

  • Samaritans of Singapore (SOS) - 24/7 emotional support and suicide prevention services. Hotline: 1767
  • Community Health Assessment Team (CHAT) - Mental health resources and assessment for youths aged 16 to 30.
  • Silver Ribbon (Singapore) - Promotes mental health awareness and offers support services. Hotline: +65 6386 1928
  • REACH Community Services - Counseling and mental wellness services for families, youth, and seniors.

Find a Therapist in Singapore

Explore a directory of licensed therapists in Singapore. Contact them directly for more information on services and appointments.

  • Singapore Association for Counselling - Directory of licensed counselors in Singapore.
  • Singapore Psychological Society - Find certified psychologists in various specializations.
  • BetterHelp - Access online therapy sessions with licensed professionals.
